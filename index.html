About Me

Darren "JJ" Jenkins-Johnston is an actor, singer, and emerging filmmaker based in Washington, D.C. and Chicago, IL. He brings grounded intensity, humor, and natural charisma to every role across stage and screen.

Currently pursuing a BA in Theatre with a Minor in Film & Media Studies at Northwestern University (Dean's List, 6 consecutive quarters), JJ has also earned Technical Diplomas in Filmmaking and Acting for Film from the Prague Film School. His credits include originating the role of Jarred Litke in TikTok (a period piece) at Round House Theatre, and playing Ben in the feature film Thankskilling Day (Bontonfilm, 2026).

Beyond acting, JJ enjoys taking long walks, soccer, good food, and spending time with loved ones.
